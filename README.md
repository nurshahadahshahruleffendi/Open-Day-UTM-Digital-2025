# Open Day UTM Digital 2025

Date : 28 October 2025
I had an opportunity to attend Open Day UTM Digital 2025 and gain an insight into UTMSmart app development and digital infrastructure at UTM.

I learnt how UTMSmart is transforming campus services through integrated information systems and continuously improving by adding features that are helpful to students, from students' admissions to graduation. This talk shows the importance of combining technical knowledge with practical application in digital services to help studentsâ€™ daily lives be easier.
